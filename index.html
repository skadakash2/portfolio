Publications

Published work in sustainable food systems and food science

Food Biomacromolecules | Apr 9, 2026

Edible coatings in food: Specific materials and their impact on techno-functional properties and sustainable applications

A review article exploring edible coatings, techno-functional properties, and sustainable applications for modern packaging challenges.

This review summarizes commonly used edible coating materials (polysaccharides, proteins, lipids), evaluates their effects on barrier and mechanical properties, and discusses sustainable sourcing and end-of-life considerations. It also highlights applications where edible coatings improve shelf-life and reduce packaging waste.

Food & Beverages Processing | Nov 5, 2024

Anti-nutritional factors in millets and methods to mitigate them

A publication focused on anti-nutritional factors in millets and practical mitigation methods for better food processing outcomes.

The paper reviews common anti-nutritional compounds in millets (tannins, phytates, enzyme inhibitors) and presents thermal, fermentation, and soaking strategies that reduce their levels while preserving nutritional value and functional properties for industrial processing.
